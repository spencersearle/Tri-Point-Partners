About Mini Courses

Personalized Coaching, Not Cookie-Cutter Content

Our mini courses are one-on-one coaching sessions with individual Tri-Point Partners. Unlike traditional courses, these are built around you — your role, your challenges, your goals, and your school context.

Each session combines focused instruction, guided reflection, and practical application. You'll leave with strategies you can put to work immediately — not theory to file away and forget.

Sessions are available virtually or in person and can be scheduled as one-time deep dives or ongoing coaching engagements.
