EP 1

Pilot Episode — Who We Are & Why We Do This

In the first episode of Triangle Talks, Ryan Searle, Jacinda Waldrip, and Ryan Puckett introduce themselves and share the journey that brought them together at Rawlins Middle School — and ultimately to Tri-Point Partners. They cover their individual career paths, the origin of the "Triangle" nickname their staff gave them, the grind of turning a struggling school into a Model PLC, and the personal missions that keep them rooted in education. Equal parts honest, funny, and real.
